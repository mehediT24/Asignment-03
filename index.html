Clients Question

Here are some common questions from our clients. If you have a question not listed here, feel free to contact us.

What are the key features to look for in a hockey stick?

When choosing a hockey stick, consider factors such as the material (wood, composite), flex, length, and blade curve. Look for a stick that suits your playing style and skill level.

How do I select the right size of hockey skates?

To select the right size of hockey skates, measure the length and width of your foot and refer to the manufacturer's sizing chart. Consider factors such as comfort, fit, and support.

What are the benefits of using hockey protective gear?

Hockey protective gear, such as helmets, shoulder pads, elbow pads, and shin guards, provides essential protection against injuries during gameplay. It reduces the risk of concussions, fractures, and bruises.

How can I maintain my hockey equipment for longevity?

To maintain your hockey equipment, regularly clean and dry it after each use. Inspect for any signs of wear and tear, and repair or replace damaged parts. Store your equipment in a dry and ventilated area to prevent odor and mold buildup.
